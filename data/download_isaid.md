\# iSAID Dataset Download Instructions



\## Official Dataset

The iSAID dataset can be downloaded from:

\- \*\*Official Website\*\*: https://captain-whu.github.io/iSAID/

\- \*\*Paper\*\*: https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.12886



\## Download Steps



\### Option 1: Direct Download

1\. Visit: https://captain-whu.github.io/iSAID/dataset.html

2\. Register and request access

3\. Download the following files:

&nbsp;  - `train.zip` (8.7 GB)

&nbsp;  - `val.zip` (1.3 GB)

&nbsp;  - `test.zip` (1.3 GB)

&nbsp;  - `annotations.zip` (300 MB)
